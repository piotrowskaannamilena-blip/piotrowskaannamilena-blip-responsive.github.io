About us

Welcome to Polish Pete RC!

I'm Pete – better known in the RC world as Polish Pete – a passionate RC enthusiast with a deep love for all things fast, custom, and remote controlled. What started as a weekend hobby has grown into a full-blown obsession, and now a dedicated platform to share that passion with others.

Whether it’s tearing up the track, building custom rigs, reviewing the latest gear, or just getting my hands dirty in the workshop, I’m all about pushing the limits and having fun while doing it. Through Polish Pete RC, I aim to connect with fellow hobbyists, inspire newcomers, and showcase the creativity and engineering behind this incredible hobby.

Thanks for stopping by – and whether you're here to learn, collaborate, or just geek out over RC – you're in the right place.

Stay tuned, stay fast,
Polish Pete
