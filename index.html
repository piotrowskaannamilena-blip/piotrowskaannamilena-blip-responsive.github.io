1/5th Scale RC Car Services

These are the big beasts of the RC world – and they demand serious attention. We offer full servicing for 1/5th scale gas, petrol, or electric RC cars. From engine tuning and rebuilds to suspension upgrades, bodywork customization, and full diagnostics – we've got the tools and expertise to keep your large-scale rig performing like a champ.

1/8th Scale RC Car Services

Perfect for racing and high-performance bashing, 1/8th scale RC cars require precise setup and regular maintenance. We offer nitro and electric motor tuning, chassis balancing, drivetrain overhauls, and electronics installation. Whether you're competing or just bashing hard, we'll help you get peak performance.

1/10th Scale RC Car Services

Small but mighty! 1/10th scale cars are perfect for beginners, racers, and hobbyists alike. Our services include electronics setup, suspension tuning, bearing replacement, motor upgrades, and complete rebuilds. We also offer beginner-friendly support and troubleshooting.
